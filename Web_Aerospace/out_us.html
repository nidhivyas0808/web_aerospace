ABOUT
AEROSPACE

AEROSPACE is one of the premier skills
development training institutes in India.
Since its inception in 1993,
it has been consistently growing and is today recognised as
the World's No. 1 Air Hostess Training Institute. With a unique curriculum,
pedagogy and innovative programs,
it has chartered a unique and high growth path
not only in Aviation but also
in Hospitality, Travel management and Customer service.
